NBIAD-8: A Hybrid Deep Learning Approach with Interpretable Insights to Detect Pedestrian Non-Compliance Behavior for Road Safety

- Overview: I have worked under the supervision of "Prof. Dr. MohammadNurul Huda" to developed a hybrid Deep Learning pipeline that is able to detect pedestrian non-compliance behavior in urban traffic environments to mitigate hazardous outcomes enhancing road safety.
